Who We Are

Specialized agricultural exporters dedicated to quality and sustainability

Excellence in Every Export

Bianco Farm Exports is a specialized agricultural exporter based in Kenya, dedicated exclusively to supplying fresh herbs and premium avocados to international markets. We work directly with certified smallholder farmers and commercial growers under controlled cultivation systems — ensuring traceability, freshness, and compliance with global food safety standards.

We do not import or distribute locally. Our sole focus: exporting high-quality, sustainably grown produce from Kenya to your kitchen.

With our farm-to-port traceability system and cold-chain integrity, we guarantee that every product maintains its peak freshness and quality from harvest to delivery.
